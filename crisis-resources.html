Community Support Resources

Suicide Crisis Helpline (Canada)

1-866-522-2122 Veterans
1-866-522-2022 Serving members

National Defence / CAF Member Assistance Program
1-800-268-7708

Immediate counselling, crisis support, and psychological services for serving members & families.

24/7 emotional support, crisis guidance, and family-specific resources.

1-888-706-4808

Trauma counselling, mental health programs, and family support services.
